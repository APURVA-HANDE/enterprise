About us


Established in 1994, Mehta Pressing is a renowned sheet metal engineering and manufacturing company. Our team of skilled professionals, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and globally recognized processes have made us a preferred player in the engineering and mobility industry. With expertise in production, procurement, and process management, we prioritize on-time delivery and superior quality, solidifying our position as a global leader in this field.

Vision

“Become a world-class sheet metal supplier to fulfill all customer requirements from various sectors under one roof.”
